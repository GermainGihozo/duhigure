Our Story

DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is a family-based performance contract system initiated by Kibungo Sector to enhance development planning and accountability at household level.

The program aligns family goals with national development strategies by assigning performance duties (Imihigo) to families and individual members. It represents a grassroots approach to achieving Rwanda's Vision 2050 through family empowerment.

Our Mission

To empower families through structured performance contracts that promote accountability, unity, and sustainable development at the household level.

Our Vision

To create a nation where every family actively participates in and contributes to Rwanda's development journey through measurable household performance.

Our Journey

2023 - Program Inception

Conceptualization of DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO program in Kibungo Sector

2024 - Pilot Phase

Successful implementation with 50 pilot families showing 75% goal achievement rate

2025 - Full Launch

Official launch and expansion to all families in Kibungo Sector with digital system

2026 - Expansion Plan

Planned expansion to Bumbogo District and other sectors nationwide

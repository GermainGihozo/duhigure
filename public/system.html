DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO Program Terms

By registering in the DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO program, you agree to:

  1. Provide accurate and truthful information about your family
  2. Participate actively in setting and achieving Imihigo
  3. Update progress regularly in the system
  4. Allow sector officials to verify your progress
  5. Use the system for its intended purpose of family development
  6. Respect the privacy and data of other families

Your data will be protected according to Rwanda's data protection laws and will only be used for program purposes.

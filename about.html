About DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO

DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is a family-based performance contract system initiated by Kibungo Sector to enhance development planning and accountability at household level.

The program aligns family goals with national development strategies by assigning performance duties (Imihigo) to families and individual members.

Beneficiaries

  • Families
  • Local Leaders
  • Sector Administration
  • National Development Programs

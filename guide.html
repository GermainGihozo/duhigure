How the DUHIGURE System Works

  1. Family information is registered in the system.
  2. Each family member is recorded with full personal details.
  3. Individual performance duties are assigned to members.
  4. Shared family performance duties are added.
  5. Progress is monitored by authorities and community facilitators.

Registration can be done by the family, assisted by an Intore mu Ikoranabuhanga, or by an administrative authority.

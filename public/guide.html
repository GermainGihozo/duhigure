How the System Works

1

Family Information Registration

Register your family in the system with basic information including:

  • Family name and head of family
  • National ID number of the head
  • Contact information (phone number)
  • Location (sector, cell, village)
2

Family Member Registration

Add all family members with their details:

  • Full name and relationship to head
  • Age and gender
  • Role in the family
  • Education level and occupation
3

Set Performance Duties (Imihigo)

Define clear performance duties for:

  • Individual Duties: Specific responsibilities for each member
  • Family Duties: Shared responsibilities for all members
  • Time-bound: Set target completion dates
  • Measurable: Clear criteria for success
4

Progress Monitoring

Track and update progress regularly:

  • Update completion status of duties
  • Upload evidence (photos, documents)
  • Receive feedback from authorities
  • Monitor overall family performance
5

Verification and Certification

Complete the performance cycle:

  • Authorities verify completed duties
  • Receive performance certificates
  • Qualify for incentives and recognition
  • Set new goals for next period

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register in DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO?

Any family residing in Kibungo Sector can register. Currently, the program is available to all families in Kibungo, with plans for expansion to other sectors.

Is there a cost to register?

No, registration is completely free for all families. The program is funded by Kibungo Sector to promote community development.

What types of Imihigo can we set?

Imihigo can include various areas: economic development, education, health, agriculture, family unity, savings, home improvement, and community service.

How often should we update progress?

We recommend updating progress at least monthly. However, you can update anytime you achieve a milestone or complete a duty.

Can we modify Imihigo after setting them?

Yes, you can modify Imihigo with approval from your sector authority if circumstances change. Contact your sector office for modifications.

What are the benefits of participating?

Benefits include: improved family unity, access to sector support, recognition certificates, priority in some development programs, and contributing to community development.
